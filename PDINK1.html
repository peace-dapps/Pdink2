Frequently Asked Questions

PickleDink (PDINK) is the utility token that powers the PicklePayouts ecosystem, a platform for pickleball players to compete, wager, and earn rewards on the Solana blockchain using the Radium token standard.

PDINK is built on the Solana blockchain, which offers high speed and low transaction costs. It uses the Radium token standard within the Solana ecosystem.

PDINK is used for tournament entry fees, match wagering, earning rewards, marketplace purchases, and eventually community governance on the PicklePayouts platform.

PDINK is available on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) on the Solana network. You can track prices and trade on platforms like Raydium and Jupiter.

The PickleDink Platform is currently in development and is scheduled for release between Q4 2025 and Q1 2026. It will allow users to connect wallets, view balances, and participate in tournaments.

PDINK has a fixed total supply of 100,000,000 tokens, all of which were released at minting with no inflationary mechanism.
